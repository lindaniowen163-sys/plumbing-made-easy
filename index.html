Questions
FAQ
Do I need a smartphone?+
Any smartphone works — iPhone or Android. You open the app in your browser and add it to your home screen. No App Store needed, no downloads.
Does it work without internet?+
The app loads and displays your jobs even without internet. For saving new jobs or sending invoices you'll need a connection — but basic viewing works offline.
What happens after 14 days?+
After your free trial you'll be prompted to subscribe for R499/month. No automatic charges — you choose when to subscribe. Your data is kept safe either way.
Can I use it for my whole team?+
The current plan covers one plumber per account. Multi-user team features are coming soon. If you have a team, contact us and we'll sort you out.
How do I cancel?+
Cancel anytime from the Settings screen inside the app. No notice period, no penalties. Your data can be exported before you go.
Is my data safe?+
Your business data is private to your account. No one else can see your jobs or invoices. We never sell your data to anyone.
